Tree Plantation Program

Love Green Nepal (LGN) established with the slogan of ‘Let’s Green Our Earth’ in 1991 A.D. The main objective is to enhance greenery development and mitigate the environmental problems comprises deforestation, landslides, drying up of natural water sources as well as to enhance the farmer’s income. In initial years, it started from Sankhu area of Kathmandu District. After the successful implementation of this program, LGN selected Panchkhal valley of Kavrepalanchok District of Nepal for the massive tree plantation. Under this program, LGN carried out a community-based tree plantation campaign and distributes fuelwood, fruits, fodder tree seedlings to the farmers, and conducts an awareness program.
